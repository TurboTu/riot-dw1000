◆ ADC_MAX_CLK

#define ADC_MAX_CLK   (4000000U)

Maximum clock speed.

The ADC clock speed must be configured to be >2MHz and <12MHz in order for the ADC to work. For ADC calibration to work as intended, the ADC clock speed must further no be >4MHz, so we target a ADC clock speed > 2 and <4MHz.
