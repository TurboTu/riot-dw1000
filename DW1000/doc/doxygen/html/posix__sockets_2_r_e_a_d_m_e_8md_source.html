1 examples/posix_sockets
2 ======================
3 This application is a showcase for RIOT's POSIX socket support. To
4 keep things simple this application has only one-hop support and
5 no routing capabilities.
6 
7 Usage
8 =====
9 
10 Build, flash and start the application:
11 ```
12 export BOARD=your_board
13 make
14 make flash
15 make term
16 ```
17 
18 The `term` make target starts a terminal emulator for your board. It
19 connects to a default port so you can interact with the shell, usually
20 that is `/dev/ttyUSB0`. If your port is named differently, the
21 `PORT=/dev/yourport` (not to be confused with the UDP port) variable can
22 be used to override this.
