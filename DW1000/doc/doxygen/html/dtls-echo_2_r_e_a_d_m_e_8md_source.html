9 ## SOCKET vs. Non-socket (GNRC)
10 
11 This example is configured to use the GNRC instead of sockets (over GNRC).
12 At the moment, the configuration must be done manually in the Makefile of
13 this project.

15 ## Fast configuration (Between RIOT instances):
16 
17 Preparing the logical interfaces:
18 
19  ./../../dist/tools/tapsetup/tapsetup --create 2
20 
21 For the server instance:
22 
23  make all; PORT=tap1 make term
24  dtlss start
25  ifconfig
26 
27 Do not forget to copy the IPv6 addresses!
28 
29 For the client:
30 
31  PORT=tap0 make term
32  dtlsc <IPv6's server address> "DATA TO DATA TO DATA!"
